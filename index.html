Strixhaven University

Where Magic Meets Mastery

LOREHOLD

COLLEGE OF ARCHAEOMANCY

Lorehold students are diligent researchers, daring adventurers, and scholars passionate about history. They explore the past by poring over artifacts and summoning long-dead spirits. For some, the past is a rich tapestry for disciplined study. For Lorehold it's a cape worn as they jump into a forgotten tomb.

PRISMARI

COLLEGE OF ELEMENTAL ARTS

These students' spells are spectacles of raw creativity or meticulous artistic expressions. All the world’s a stage, and whether informed by mind or emotion, Prismari always leave a lasting impression.

QUANDRIX

COLLEGE OF NUMEROMANCY

Quandrix mages are ingenious math magicians. They study patterns, fractals, and symmetries to command power over the fundamental forces of nature. They can simultaneously contemplate the metaphysical properties of the universe while reciting every number of Pi backwards.

SILVERQUILL

COLLEGE OF ELOQUENCE

Wielding the magic of words, Silverquill students are stylish, intimidating, and tirelessly competitive. These mages are born leaders with razor-sharp wit and natural charisma that can be used for good or for ill.

WITHERBLOOM

COLLEGE OF LIFE AND DEATH

Witherbloom students draw power from the essence of living beings, whether that means enhancing nature or exploiting it. Witherbloom mages are most at home riding zombie crocs, picking herbs for potions, and hanging out in their swamp making grim jokes.

Lorehold – College of Archaeomancy

“Leave no stone unturned.”

Scholars who combine history with elemental power to reawaken the past.

Prerequisite Knowledge:

Basic magical history, elemental spellcasting, research techniques.

Working Methods:

Archive expeditions, lecture-based theory, spell reenactments, interactive artifact studies.

Your Profile:

Inquisitive, detail-oriented thinkers passionate about uncovering ancient truths and wielding power responsibly.

Annual Division & Exams:

  • Year 2: Magical historiography, elemental basics.
  • Year 3: Practical archeomancy labs, timeline manipulation.
  • Year 4: Expedition practicum, thesis defense (oral + written), ritual reconstruction exam.

Prismari – College of Elemental Arts

“Express yourself with fire and ice.”

Art and magic collide in spectacular displays, where creativity fuels elemental energy.

Prerequisite Knowledge:

Fundamentals of elemental control, art or performance background, emotional regulation skills.

Working Methods:

Studio labs, expressive casting sessions, live performances, immersive dueling workshops.

Your Profile:

Passionate, expressive individuals who see the world as a canvas for their inner magic.

Annual Division & Exams:

  • Year 2: Introduction to artistic spellcasting.
  • Year 3: Medium-specific elemental mastery (fire, ice, lightning, etc.).
  • Year 4: Public magical showcase, artist’s magical thesis, faculty juried duel performance.

Quandrix – College of Numeromancy

“Math is magic.”

Quandrix harnesses numbers, fractals, and theories to shape reality itself.

Prerequisite Knowledge:

Algebraic spellcraft, logic theory, dimensional awareness.

Working Methods:

Abstract lectures, computational spell labs, dimensional simulation domes.

Your Profile:

Analytical minds with a love for logic, structure, and bending rules through formulas.

Annual Division & Exams:

  • Year 2: Magical calculus, spell matrices.
  • Year 3: Fractal mechanics, probability-based conjuration.
  • Year 4: Independent numerical thesis, multidimensional geometry trial, formulaic spell combat.

Silverquill – College of Eloquence

“Say the perfect thing at the perfect time.”

Masters of rhetoric, persuasion, and shadow magic, Silverquill shapes minds—and fate—through words.

Prerequisite Knowledge:

Basic rhetoric, persuasive speech, introductory shadow magic.

Working Methods:

Public speaking workshops, shadow casting duels, poetry slams, rhetorical combat labs.

Your Profile:

Charismatic and witty students who influence, lead, and reshape the world through language.

Annual Division & Exams:

  • Year 2: Spellbinding syntax, verbal spell duel techniques.
  • Year 3: Dark ink manipulation, persuasive enchantments.
  • Year 4: Final oratorical battle, personal credo essay, published shadow-manifested poem.

Witherbloom – College of Essence Studies

“Get your hands dirty.”

Witherbloom blends biology and necromancy, exploring the tension between life and decay.

Prerequisite Knowledge:

Herbology, potion basics, magical anatomy.

Working Methods:

Fieldwork in magical swamps, potion labs, spirit harvesting, nature walks with arcane lectures.

Your Profile:

Empathetic, curious, and unafraid of the darker side of nature—often drawn to healing or sustainable magic.

Annual Division & Exams:

  • Year 2: Botanical familiars, potion theory.
  • Year 3: Vivisection practicum, life-force transmutation.
  • Year 4: Major brew portfolio, swamp trials (practical), final ethical necromancy report.
